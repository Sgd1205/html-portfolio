The Best Movies According To Sharon

My top 3 movies of all time:

Jab we met


While they miss their train, Geet and Aditya begin a journey together to her home and what follows is a love that changes them.
Favorite Bollywood movie of all time (Comfort movie).

Barfi


Explores themes of love, friendship, sacrifice, and acceptance. It follows the story of Murphy "Barfi" Johnson (Ranbir Kapoor), a deaf-mute young man who falls in love with two women, Shruti (Ileana D'Cruz) and Jhilmil (Priyanka Chopra).
Had me smiling sobbing laughing basically a whole roller coaster ride of emotions.

Harry Potter (whole movie series)


Doesn't even need a explanation- Iconic !!
